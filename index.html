Welcome to the UMSI Career Development Resource Hub

Your Central Hub for Career Success at UMSI

Ready to take the next step toward your future career? The UMSI Career Development Resource Hub is your one-stop shop for trusted guidance, practical tools, and the latest information designed specifically for School of Information students.

Navigating the job market can feel overwhelming, but you don’t have to do it alone. Whether you’re crafting your first professional resume, seeking a summer internship, or looking to grow your professional network, we’ve gathered the best resources in one place to help you succeed.

Explore Top UMSI Career Pathways

Our students go on to shape the future of information across a wide variety of industries. Here are some of the most common fields our graduates enter:

  • User Experience (UX) Design & Research
    Create intuitive, accessible, and engaging digital products. Roles include UX Designer, Product Designer, and User Researcher.
  • Data Science & Analytics
    Turn complex datasets into actionable insights. Roles include Data Analyst, Data Scientist, and Machine Learning Engineer.
  • Library & Archival Science
    Preserve history and make information accessible to communities. Roles include Academic Librarian, Archivist, and Digital Preservationist.
  • Information Policy & Management
    Navigate the ethical, legal, and strategic use of technology. Roles include Privacy Consultant, Product Manager, and Policy Analyst.

How to Use This Hub

Explore our dedicated sections to find exactly what you need:

  • Resumes: Access templates, rubrics, and guides to build a standout resume.
  • Networking: Learn how to make meaningful connections and communicate your value.
  • Internship Search: Discover where to find opportunities and how to land them.

Our mission is to empower you with the skills and confidence to pursue your professional goals. Explore the site, book a coaching session, or just say hello.

Beyond the Hub: Meet the CDO Team

While this digital hub is available 24/7, the Career Development Office (CDO) offers personalized, in-person, and virtual support to all UMSI students.

We host regular workshops, bring top employers directly to campus for recruiting events, and offer one-on-one coaching sessions. Whether you are completely lost and need to brainstorm career paths, or you have a final round interview tomorrow and need to practice, our dedicated team is here to support your unique journey.

Visit our Canvas page to learn more about our coaching services.

Getting Started FAQ

  • I'm a first-year student. Is it too early to use the CDO?
    Never! It is highly recommended to start exploring your options, building your resume, and attending events during your first year. Establishing a foundation early makes the internship search much smoother.
  • Do I have to know what career I want before making an appointment?
    Absolutely not. Our career coaches specialize in helping you explore your interests, assess your skills, and discover potential career paths you might not have considered.
